Design principles
Six decisions that shape the optimized process.
01
Minimum viable process
Concept Development and Business Case are merged. Build, Launch Prep and Launch are unified into one delivery phase with a readiness checkpoint before go-live.
02
Both gates are non-negotiable
Filtering before business case investment and before build investment delivers the highest ROI of any process element. Both gates are retained and strengthened.
03
Governance from Policy v1.0
The Idea Governance Advisory Committee — CFO-chaired, ~55% external votes, conflict-of-interest rules, quarterly monitoring role — is adopted wholesale.
04
Implementation depth from the Guide
Agile/Scrum, the 24hr/48hr escalation chain, three complexity tiers, and the formal launch readiness confirmation are all carried forward.
05
KPIs at every phase
Process A had no per-stage metrics. The optimized process defines measurable outcomes at every step, making performance visible and improvement actionable.
06
Continuous loop, not a project
Monitor & Improve feeds validated insights directly back into Strategic Ideation — making innovation an operating discipline rather than a one-off exercise.
